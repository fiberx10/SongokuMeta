GOKU Token is an Ethereum based token under the ERC20 standard.

The premise of the token GOKU is utility based, designed to route the SonGoku platforms Marketplace, pre-built solution, SonGoku Computing, and SonGoku Learn. The GOKU token will be created as a major currency in which community participants use to cash in and out of transactions taking place on the platform. It is compatible with third-party service wallets, exchanges etc, and provides easy-to-use integration.

GOKU tokens are directly involved in transactions taking place on the SonGoku

The number of GOKU tokens issued is fixed and no more tokens can be ever issued, which is guaranteed by the GOKU Token Smart Contract. GOKU tokens are directly involved in transactions taking place on the SonGoku platform. Any unsold tokens shall be retained by SonGoku and allocated to Reward Pool to grow decentralised computing resources connected to the SonGoku ecosystem, its community and partners.

Token Usage in Transactions
The SonGoku platform accepts payments in both Cryptocurrency (GOKU Token) and fiat currencies. Users are able to pay directly for transactions via fiat currency and/or are given the option to load their wallets with a utility token GOKU to take advantage of incentives provided in the platform.
For AI Experts
Those who continue to use and populate the platform are continuously given incentives to adopt the GOKU Tokens as a means to strengthen the community. When receiving GOKU, AI professionals are able to use the token (internally) for various elements, potentially strengthening their profiles. Apart from Token incentives, AI experts are given a chance to earn more money due by adhering to a platform adopted token and moving away from currency exchange rates associated with competitor sites.
For Clients
Clients are automatically plugged into token adoption as soon as they complete KYC and platform sign up. From that moment on, all transactions and job biddings are offered at discounted prices if the option to pay with GOKU Token. To accrue GOKU, users must load their wallets and process actions accordingly or take part in other platform opportunities that incentivize them (ex. Complete your profile is worth 10 GOKU). In any case that a user will want to cash out their wallet, they will be given the same incentives as AI experts to cash out via selected exchanges.
